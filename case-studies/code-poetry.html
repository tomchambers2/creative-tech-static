Codepoetry

Codepoetry is an urban poetry game. Scattered QR codes invite you to discover the poetry hidden in unexpected places. With the help of a smartphone you scan the QR code you found and it leads you to a random poem from the constantly updated online collection. Visit the project's site for more information.

Collaborators

Codepoetry is the result of our collaborative effort with the poet/translator/hacker Ioannis Dimitriadis and it's been running in the streets since 2011.
